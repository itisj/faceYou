Face You

Allow the browser to access your webcam, Right Click to Snap

Would the visually identicality be the original, the first abstraction of oneself?
What about that identical image of oneself juxtaposing with other abstractions
in the browser? Will this visual abstraction of oneself along with simple time
and date become the second? Or it is already the second abstraction from the first place?

My work might not be able to answer those questions, the intention here is more about asking them.
